How It Works

Our language detection system uses advanced statistical modeling techniques to identify the language of any given text with high accuracy.

N-gram Analysis

The system breaks down text into sequences of characters (n-grams) and compares their frequency distributions against known language profiles.

Probability-Based Approach

Using Bayesian probability, the system calculates the likelihood that the text belongs to each candidate language and selects the most probable one.

Machine Learning Integration

Our models are trained on extensive multilingual datasets, allowing them to recognize subtle patterns and distinguish between similar languages.
